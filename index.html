An electric future of flight

We are decarbonizing the turboprop category by developing proprietary electrified powertrain technology. As aircraft become cheaper to operate with electrification, we believe we can catalyze new routes to create a new kind of point-to-point air travel that can replace busy highways and large crowded airports.

More Information

We are developing proprietary electrified powertrains that will catalyze the next generation of air travel. Our electrified powertrain will be first integrated with one of the world’s most popular turboprop aircraft, the Cessna Grand Caravan. By creating a solution to upgrade existing aircraft, we plan to bring the benefits of electric to market as quickly as possible through a Supplemental Type Certificate (“STC”). Our key partnerships with magniX and AeroTEC, two industry leaders in aviation innovation, will help accelerate our path to commercialization. And our exclusive relationship with Textron Aviation, the world’s largest general aviation OEM, to electrify the Cessna Grand Caravan will help us turn this vehicle into a mass transportation platform. Furthermore, by starting with hybrid electric powertrains that don’t require charging infrastructure, our electrified Cessna Grand Caravans can fly between any airport. Our future generation aircraft will expand the scope of mission profiles and use-cases even further as our technology improves and we create new solutions for a continually broader spectrum of larger aircraft types.
